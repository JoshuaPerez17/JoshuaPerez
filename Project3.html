What is this page?

This is the webpage of the University of Cyprus Neuroscience lab. Our team consists of Phd accredited researchers from various universities, who are also experienced in the fields of Psychology, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Applied Neuroscience.

What do we do?

The University of Cyprus Neuroscience Lab is devoted to research of brain development, cognition, and stimuli. We have published research on the similarities between mental rotation and egocentric perspective talking, how the brain is stimulated by movie trailers and other media, and the potential of nonintrusive BCI systems. Our goal is to discover the various ways the brain to entertainment, figuring out how similar actions are performed by different sections of the brain, and how we can study the brain without heavy BCI equipment.
